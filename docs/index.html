Why klipa

Ultralight

A 1.3 MB binary using ~10 MB RAM - no Electron, no browser engine, no GPU renderer.

Menubar dropdown

Click the menubar icon and your recent copies drop down. Click one to paste it. That is the whole UI.

Local-only, private

Your copies stay in one local file on your device. Nothing is logged, uploaded, or sent anywhere - klipa has no servers and makes no network connections.

Lives in the tray

No dock icon, no taskbar clutter. klipa stays out of the way until you need it.

Truly cross-platform

The same Rust core and native menubar dropdown on macOS, Windows, and Linux.

Open source

MIT licensed. Read it, build it, ship it. View on GitHub.
