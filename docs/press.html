About klipa

klipa is a native menu bar clipboard manager for macOS, Windows and Linux. Every copy is kept as one entry in a native menu bar dropdown - text and images alike - and clicking any entry copies it back so you can paste it. klipa also offers keep-awake sessions to stop your machine from sleeping, and can optionally show the date, the current temperature at your location, or both next to its status bar icon.

klipa is about 3.6 MB (roughly 6 MB installed) and uses about 25 MB of RAM. It is written in pure Rust with no Electron, no WebView and no JavaScript runtime. Your clipboard history stays on your device: klipa has no accounts, no cloud sync and no telemetry. It is MIT licensed, with a 7-day free trial and a one-time €1.99 unlock, and is also available on the Mac App Store, Homebrew, Scoop, winget and AUR.
